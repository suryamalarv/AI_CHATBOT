leave policy: Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves and 6 sick leaves per year.

sick leaves: You can take up to 6 sick leaves per calendar year.

working hours: Employees should work 8 hours per day between 9 AM and 6 PM with a 1-hour break.

dress code: Formal attire from Monday to Thursday, casuals on Friday.

maternity leave: Female employees can avail 6 months of maternity leave.

paternity leave: Male employees can take 10 days of paternity leave.

salary credit: Salary is credited on the last working day of every month.

payroll issues: For any payroll issues, please contact hr@company.com.

public holidays: There are 12 public holidays every year, including Republic Day, Independence Day, and Diwali.

late coming: Employees arriving more than 15 minutes late thrice a month will receive a warning notice.
